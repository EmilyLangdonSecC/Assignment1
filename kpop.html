K-Pop

I've always liked music, but it wasn't until middle school that I started actively listening to artists I like. It all started with a friend of mine who was into K-pop. She introduced me to these groups who I still listen to to this day!


This song is called Lucifer, by a group called SHINee. It's one of the first K-pop songs I remember listening to. To this day, SHINee is one of my favourite musical artists. Their style has evolved since Lucifer came out in 2010, but I enjoy their whole discography!

Though not my first, this will always be one of my favourite BTS songs. It's called Spring Day. I got into BTS roughly around the same time as SHINee, and while their music is very different, I love both groups. I even went to a BTS concert in 2018!

In my humble opinion, this song is one of the most iconic K-pop songs in existence. It's called Fantastic Baby, by a group called BigBang. I can't remember what my first BigBang song was, but I know that I've loved their music since listening to it. This group was a pioneer in the K-pop industry.
