Ghost

This page is dedicated entirely to the band Ghost, who I discovered in August 2023. It is debatable whether they are considered metal, but they are unequivocally my favourite band.

Remember the song from the Home page? That was these guys! Though it's worth noting that Ghost is less of a band and more of a project orchestrated by Tobias Forge, who is the main man under the mask in the above photo.

Have a listen to what is often considered the quintessential Ghost song while I rank the albums based on my personal enjoyment! (I love all of their albums, of course.)

  1. Meliora
  2. Impera
  3. Infestissumam
  4. Opus Eponymous
  5. Prequelle

Behold, my favourite Ghost song! Please enjoy listening to Mummy Dust.
